About

Welcome to Accent & Speech, where Dianne Holly, a dedicated speech pathologist with over 20 years of experience, helps clients gain confidence in their communication skills. Specializing in accent modification and speech clarity, Dianne provides personalized, one-on-one sessions tailored to each individual's needs.

Whether you're a professional looking to enhance your speech, an actor refining pronunciation, or simply someone who wants to speak with greater clarity and confidence, Dianne’s approach ensures effective and supportive coaching. Sessions are conducted online via Zoom, making expert speech therapy accessible from anywhere.

Through structured exercises, practical strategies, and expert guidance, Dianne helps clients achieve clearer, more natural speech patterns. Book a session today and take the first step toward speaking with confidence!
